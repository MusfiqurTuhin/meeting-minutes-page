## Key Discussion Points

### 1. Page Review & Positive Content Strategy

**Objective:** Establish a review system that highlights the organization's positive work effectively.

**Key Decisions:**
- All content must be positive and inspiring
- Highlight the good work done for the community
- Ensure all posts align with organizational values
- Maintain consistency in messaging and tone

### 2. Page Category Changes

| Previous Category | New Categories |
|---|---|
| Social Service | 1. Community Organization<br>2. Political Organization<br>3. Nonprofit Organization |

**Rationale:** Multiple categories increase visibility and clearly communicate the nature of our work to a broader audience.

### 3. Look & Feel - Visual Changes

#### Profile & Cover Photo
- Update profile picture with professional, high-quality image
- Design and set new cover photo
- Ensure brand color and style consistency across all platforms

#### Banner Updates
- Update banners to match photocard design style
- Maintain consistent look across Facebook, groups, and pages
- Ensure professional appearance everywhere

### 4. Social Media Monitoring & Trending Topics

**Strategy:** Regularly monitor trending topics on Facebook for engagement opportunities.

**Guidelines:**
- Monitor current trending topics and conversations
- Create relevant content based on trending topics to increase engagement
- Ensure all content remains positive and adds value
- Avoid negative or controversial content

### 5. Page Engagement Growth

**Team Responsibilities:**
- Add the page to personal "Favorites"
- Invite all friends to follow the page
- Leverage personal networks for organic growth
- Regularly like, comment, and share page posts

### 6. Content Quality Maintenance

**Commitment:** High-quality content across all platforms

**Guidelines:**
- Thoughtfully write and organize posts
- Proofread for spelling and grammar before posting
- Share posts in related Facebook groups for broader reach
- Maintain consistent tone of voice everywhere

### 7. Daily Photocard Posting Initiative

**Plan:** Establish a fixed routine for daily photocard posts.

**Requirements:**
- Define fixed number of daily photocards
- Track posting count
- Monitor response to each card
- Adjust strategy based on performance

### 8. Best Posting Times (Peak Hours)

**Optimal Time Slot:** 4:00 PM - 9:00 PM

**Implementation:**
- Post all important content within this timeframe
- Schedule live sessions and major events during peak hours
- Team should be active during this window to maximize reach

### 9. Editorial Guidelines Update

**Task:** Refine and update posting policy (Guideline #6)

**Focus Areas:**
- Update guidelines document
- Clarify posting rules and procedures
- Define content approval process
- Create "Best Practices" guide for the team

**Status:** Awaiting assignment
